Astros Fall Short, 6-5, at Colorado
The Colorado Rockies and Houston Astros obliged those fans who came to Colorado Field looking for a good time.

The Colorado Rockies did just enough to sneak past the Houston Astros, 6-5, at Colorado Field. Colorado first baseman Bernaldyn Bagorro earned player of the game honors. Fabien Fonzi got the win, improving to 2-0. He held the Astros to 3 earned runs on 6 hits over 5 innings, while striking out 4 and walking 3. Isaiah Holifield recorded his 3rd save for Colorado.

"Enjoyed this one," Guevara told the Colorado News. "But it's back to work tomorrow."
